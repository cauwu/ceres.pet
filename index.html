Design makes how people see the world, for better or for worse. Nobody can build the world as they see it, but the people with influence have a duty to build it right. Where a designer is tasked with building something, it has to make people truly better off; people have to be given good things that let them make the world a better place.

Designers don't have the power to change society. Voting with your dollar isn't real. But designers put things into existence that can either ruin people's lives or make them better, that can either steal from them or give them the tools to do great things. There is nothing more important than principle, duty and discipline in a designer for making sure that whatever you make, you make people healthy, happy, and able to live with dignity.
